6.2 User Manual: 
 Home Services Provider App 
Audience: Customers & Service Providers 
Platform: Android (Flutter Mobile App) 
Purpose: This manual provides a highly detailed, step-by-step guide for every screen within the app, designed for both Customers and Providers. 
👥 User Roles in this App 
Role 	Description 
Customer 	Users who browse and book home services (e.g., needing a plumber or electrician). 
Services 
Provider  	Skilled professionals who offer and perform the services. 
 
6.2.1 Splash Screen 
Purpose: The very first screen you see when you open the app. It displays the brand and checks your login status in the background. 
What Happens: 
•	The Home Services Provider App logo and branding appear on the screen. 
•	The app automatically checks if you are already logged in. 
•	If logged in: You are instantly redirected to your Dashboard. 
•	If not logged in: You are routed to the Onboarding or Sign-In screen. 
 Note: This process is fully automatic; you don't need to tap anything. 
6.2.2 Onboarding Screen 
Purpose: Introduces the app's core features to new users. This only appears the very first time you install and open the app. 
Screen Elements: 
Element 	Description 
Slide Pages 	3 to 4 swipeable screens with illustrations and short descriptions. 
Next Button 	Moves you to the next informative slide. 
Skip Button 	Skips the tutorial and takes you straight to the Sign-In screen. 
Get Started Button 	Appears on the final slide to take you to the Registration screen. 
Steps to Use: 
•	Swipe left or tap Next to read through the feature slides. 
•	If you are in a hurry, tap Skip at any time. 
•	On the last slide, tap Get Started to begin creating your account.

Screen Elements: 
Element 	Description
